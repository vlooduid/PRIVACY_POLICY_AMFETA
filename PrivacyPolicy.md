# Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all Discord bots operated by the owner of this Discord community.

## Information We Collect

Depending on the functionality of a bot, the following information may be processed and stored:

* Discord User IDs
* Discord Server IDs
* Discord Role IDs
* User balances and virtual economy data
* Inventory and virtual item ownership
* Reputation and progression statistics
* Activity statistics (messages, voice activity, command usage)
* Moderation records (warnings, mutes, bans, reports)
* Support tickets and staff applications submitted through Discord
* Invite tracking information
* Message content when required for moderation, AI assistance, security, or bot functionality

## How Information Is Used

Collected information is used exclusively for:

* Providing economy and progression systems
* Moderation and server management
* Security and anti-abuse protection
* AI-powered assistance features
* Event and community management
* Technical support and staff applications
* Statistics and activity tracking

## AI Processing

Some bots may use artificial intelligence services to provide chat assistance or detect rule violations.

Message content may be temporarily processed by third-party AI providers solely to generate responses or analyze potential violations. Conversation data is not used for advertising purposes.

## Data Storage

Data is stored in SQLite databases hosted on privately managed infrastructure controlled by the bot owner.

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored information from unauthorized access.

## Data Sharing

User information is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

Data may only be transmitted to Discord and AI service providers when required for bot functionality.

## Data Retention

Data is retained for as long as necessary to operate the bots and maintain their features.

Moderation records, economy progress, inventories, and server statistics may be retained indefinitely unless removal is requested and technically feasible.

## User Rights

Users may request information regarding stored data or request data removal by contacting server staff through the ticket system.

## Contact

For privacy-related requests, users should contact the server administration through the available support channels.
